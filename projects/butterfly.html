Butterfly Garden

VR environment, experience design

Third year | VR Class

This experience is part of a virtual square, imitating Masaryk square in the Czech republic, celebrating 50 years of international relationship between Israel and the Czech republic.

This work is a collaboration with Tal Lindenfeld. We created a memorial experience for the children's of ghetto Theresienstadt, animating their drawings, giving life to their dreams.

Every window in the maze
contains an animation
created from drawings
made by the ghetto children
