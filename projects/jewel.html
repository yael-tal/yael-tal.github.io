A catalog for an exhibition telling the story of Jewish Yemen goldsmith tradition, alongside the fascinating journey Yemenite Jews went through to immigrate to Israel, and how the jewelry-making tradition preserves itself until this day.

Yemenite jewelry craft is delicate and detailed, so I chose to bind the book by hand, in order to give a crafty feeling.
