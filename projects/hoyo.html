HOYO

Branding, digital product, AR

Third year | Concept thinking Class

HOYO is a made-up product, as a reaction for the restrictions the Covid-19 Pandemic have caused. One of the things that was forbidden is hugs. In corona times, a warm embrace can be life threatening. That is why I came up with HOYO - a hug sticker you can send someone you love, combination of a warm strap and oxytocin hormone which is naturally released while hugging.
